PROGRAMS

Scratch

INFO

This programming class is designed exclusively for beginners in grades 3-5. Participants will be introduced to fundamental programming skills such as control flow, loops, and variables using Scratch. Our teaching approach is very hands-on, with a plethora of activities that will encourage students to express their creativity. The curriculum has been carefully curated by Google, and upon completion, students will receive a certificate to commemorate their achievements!

DATES

Every Saturday from 10-11 AM from March 4th to June 10th - we hope to see you there!

Python

INFO

This programming class is tailored for beginner to intermediate programmers in grades 6-8. Using Python, participants will learn fundamental programming skills such as conditionals, loops, variables, and methods. Our class is designed to be hands-on, with a focus on building multiple games and projects that will put your skills to the test. While the class may be challenging at times, please don't feel intimidated. Overcoming challenges is a significant aspect of programming, and we are here to guide and support you throughout the course.

DATES

Every Sunday from 9-10 AM from March 5th to June 11th - we hope to see you there!

ABOUT US

From web design to AI and machine learning, programming is one of my favorite pastimes. I'm always eager to apply my skills to assist the community, particularly the younger generation.
